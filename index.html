How to Participate

To participate in our virtual poetry slam events, simply sign up on our website and submit your original poem. Make sure to follow the guidelines provided to ensure a fair evaluation by our innovative Al judging system.


---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Al Judging

Our Al judging system uses advanced algorithms to evaluate poems based on various criteria such as creativity, originality, and delivery. This ensures fair and unbiased evaluations for all participants.

Our virtual poetry slam events are scheduled periodically throughout the year. You can check our event calendar on the website to stay updated on upcoming events and their respective dates.
